# Subterranean Monastery of the Lich Lord

_The monastery of the Lich Lord is situated deep in the mountains, protected by the harsh weather and dangerous local fauna. Recently it was squatted by a party of goblins._

1. A battered door with a keyhole on the western wall.
2. Some gold under pieces of broken furniture.
3. An enchanted wand on a pedestal table.
4. A bottomless well, makes a person puke if a coin is dropped into it.
5. A hooded figure, sitting in a corner.
6. A trunk holds a bird-shaped key.
7. A carving, totally destroyed by mold.
